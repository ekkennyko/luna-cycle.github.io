# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 20, 2026**

## Overview

Luna ("the App") is a menstrual cycle tracking application. 
We built Luna with one core principle: your health data 
belongs to you, and only you.

**Luna does not collect, transmit, or store any personal 
data on external servers.**

---

## Data Storage

All data you enter in Luna — including cycle dates, 
symptoms, mood, and any other health information — is 
stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

We have no servers. We have no databases. 
We cannot access your data. Nobody can.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Health or medical data
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics tied to your identity
- Any data that leaves your device

---

## Backup

If you choose to use the optional backup feature, 
your data is encrypted on your device before being 
saved to your personal iCloud or Google Drive account. 

Luna does not have access to your backup files. 
The encryption key never leaves your device.

---

## Subscriptions

Luna uses RevenueCat to process in-app subscriptions. 
RevenueCat may collect limited transactional data 
(such as purchase receipts) as required to process 
payments. This is handled entirely by Apple App Store 
or Google Play.

We do not receive or store your payment information.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

---

## Anonymous Analytics

Luna may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage 
events (such as "app opened" or "feature used") 
through privacy-friendly analytics. These events 
cannot be tied to you personally and contain no 
health data whatsoever.

You can opt out of analytics in Settings → Privacy.

---

## App Lock

If you enable App Lock, your PIN is stored as a 
one-way cryptographic hash on your device using 
secure storage. It is never transmitted anywhere.

---

## Children

Luna is not intended for use by children under 
the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. 
We will notify you of any significant changes through 
the App. Continued use of the App after changes 
constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
